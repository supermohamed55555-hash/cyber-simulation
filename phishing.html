🔍 What Happened?

You entered your credentials into a fake login form designed to look identical to a legitimate service. The form sent your information to an attacker-controlled collection point.

❓ Why it Worked?

  • Visual Mimicry: The form used the same fonts, colors, and logos.
  • Sense of Urgency: The prompt forced you to act without thinking.

🛡️ How to Prevent it?

  • Check the URL: Always verify the domain character by character.
